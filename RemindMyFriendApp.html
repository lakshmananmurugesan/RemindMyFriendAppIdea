  • Remind My Friend APP

Hello there.

This app assists to remind things for your friend by setting reminder/alarm for your friend from your smartphone. For instance, you and your are decided to go to badminton by 7 o clock morning, for this you could alarm him on the specified time from this app. Objective is to keep actions in flow in day-to-day activities.
